USA Bombs Iran Nuclear Sites, Qatar Strike, and Trump Ceasefire in War Iran-Israel

June 24, 2025  |  By BizNews Daily

In a dramatic escalation of the war iran-israel, the USA launched "Operation Midnight Hammer" on June 22, targeting three Iranian nuclear sites—Fordow, Natanz, and Isfahan. These strikes were reportedly intended to disrupt Iran’s potential nuclear breakout capabilities.

Just hours later on June 23, Iran retaliated by launching missiles at the Al Udeid Air Base in Qatar, a major USA military installation. All incoming missiles were intercepted successfully, and no casualties were reported.

On June 24, former President Trump made a surprise announcement on Truth Social, declaring a phased ceasefire: Iran would halt its operations first, followed by Israel 12 hours later. Trump credited Qatar’s Prime Minister for facilitating talks behind the scenes.

However, Iran’s officials stated they would only honor the ceasefire if Israel did not respond with further “illegal aggression.” Sporadic missile exchanges continued even after Trump’s statement, casting doubt on the truce’s effectiveness.

What Happens Next?

  • Formal agreements between Iran and Israel on a monitored ceasefire
  • Deployment of IAEA and UN observers to sensitive regions
  • Continued mediation by Qatar, the USA, and global powers
