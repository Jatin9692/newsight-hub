1. Credit Card Spends Surge as Indians Travel More

Holiday travel helped push credit card spending to ₹1.9 lakh crore in May 2025, a 15% year-on-year rise. Over 760,000 new cards were issued in May alone, led by HDFC Bank, SBI, and Axis Bank.

  • ₹1.9 lakh crore spend (up 15% YoY)
  • 7.6 lakh new cards added
  • 110+ million cards in circulation

Business News FAQs: Credit Cards

Why did credit card spending rise in May 2025 in India?

Due to increased holiday travel, credit card usage surged to ₹1.9 lakh crore—a 15% year-on-year growth.

Which banks issued the most credit cards in May 2025?

HDFC Bank led with 274,000 new cards, followed by SBI Cards and Axis Bank.

What is the outlook for credit card growth in FY26?

Growth is expected to stabilise, with lenders focusing on quality lending and cross-selling.

2. Hindalco Buys US-based AluChem for $125 Million

Hindalco Industries is acquiring AluChem, a US-based specialty alumina firm, to strengthen its high-tech materials portfolio. This acquisition will help double capacity to 1 million tonnes and improve EBITDA margins from value-added products.

  • $125 million all-cash deal
  • 500,000 tonnes capacity → 1 million target
  • AluChem EBITDA: $381/tonne (2024)

Business News FAQs: Hindalco Acquisition

Why is Hindalco acquiring AluChem for $125 million?

To boost its specialty alumina portfolio and scale high-margin downstream operations.

What is AluChem known for?

Specialty alumina used in semiconductors, EVs, and precision ceramics.

How will the AluChem acquisition benefit Hindalco?

Increased production, better tech positioning, and higher EBITDA contribution.

3. Jaguar Land Rover to Invest ₹9,000 Cr in Tamil Nadu

JLR will assemble luxury SUVs—Range Rover & Velar—in Tamil Nadu by early 2026 under Tata Motors' plan. JLR may also move operations from Pune to this new facility. The launch of the Avinya EV is delayed to 2026.

  • ₹9,000 crore investment
  • Initial capacity: 30,000 units/year
  • Avinya launch postponed due to feasibility

Business News FAQs: JLR Investment

What will Jaguar Land Rover manufacture in Tamil Nadu?

They will initially assemble Range Rover and Velar SUVs.

Why is JLR moving production to India?

To reduce import dependency and streamline local logistics and costs.

What's the update on the Avinya EV launch?

The Avinya EV launch has been delayed until 2026 due to challenges.

4. Reliance Retail to Merge Luxe Arm for Better Efficiency

Reliance Brands—handling luxury labels like Burberry, Versace, Tiffany—will merge into Reliance Retail to eliminate overlaps and prepare for an IPO.

  • 85+ luxury brand partners
  • ₹2,684 Cr FY24 sales (up 12%)
  • 15% of RIL's EBITDA from retail

Business News FAQs: Reliance Brands

What is Reliance Brands and why is it merging with Reliance Retail?

It's the luxury arm being consolidated to drive synergy, reduce duplication, and prepare for IPO.

How many luxury labels does Reliance Brands manage?

Over 85, including top global names like Burberry, Armani, Tiffany.

What contribution does luxury retail make to Reliance's earnings?

15% of EBITDA, up from 10% four years ago.
