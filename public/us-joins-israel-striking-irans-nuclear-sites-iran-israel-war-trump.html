How Trump Ordered U.S. Strikes in the Iran-Israel War

Trump invoked executive war powers, bypassing Congress. He stated the mission "obliterated" Iran’s nuclear threat.

What Was Destroyed in Iran’s Nuclear Sites?

Precision strikes damaged uranium enrichment halls in Fordow, Natanz, and Isfahan. Nearby missile and drone factories were also hit, targeting Iran’s broader military infrastructure.

Who Was Killed in the War – Key IRGC Figures & Civilians

Why Trump and Israel Say They Hit Iran’s Nuclear Program

They argue it was to prevent Iran from acquiring nuclear weapons and to reassert deterrence. Diplomacy, they say, had failed.

Global Reactions to the Iran-Israel War Under Trump

China and Russia condemned the action. Europe called for restraint. The UN warned of nuclear safety and humanitarian risks.

What’s Next in the Iran-Israel War?
